LESSONS LEARNED: ADAPTING INTERNATIONAL PERSPECTIVES ON THE PRACTICE OF PSYCHOLOGY

This presentation will review collective efforts of psychologists integrating an international perspective on the teaching and practice of psychology. A retrospective summation of lessons learned from the perspective of Division 52 (International Psychology) of the American Psychological Association will be presented. Within in the context of an open discussion, the audience will be requested to share their international experience in adopting an international curriculum.
